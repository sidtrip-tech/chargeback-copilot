Consumer dispute preparation

Turn receipts, emails, and notes into a clearer card dispute packet.

Chargeback Copilot helps you organize legitimate dispute evidence, spot missing support, and prepare a bank-ready packet without exaggerating your case.

How it works
Careful helper

What it will not do

  • No legal or financial advice
  • No refund or dispute-win guarantees
  • No fabricated or unsupported claims

What it helps prepare

Timeline, evidence checklist, cited claims, suggested issuer message, and next steps.

How it works

Designed for legitimate disputes, not guesswork.

1. Choose the situation

Pick the closest category, like canceled subscription, non-delivery, unauthorized charge, or refund not received.

2. Add evidence

Enter receipts, emails, chat summaries, cancellation confirmations, tracking records, and issuer messages.

3. Review gaps

The app flags weak spots before you create a packet, so missing proof is visible instead of papered over.

4. Export your packet

Generate a cited packet you can review and submit through your bank or card issuer’s official process.

Common uses

Useful when the facts are real but scattered.
